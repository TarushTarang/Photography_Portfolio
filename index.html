About Me

Hi, I'm Tarush Tarang.

I’m a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing the beauty of life’s moments. My work spans portraits, landscapes, each telling its own unique story. Browse my portfolio to see my latest work.

Whether it's a candid shot of a couple in love, the serene beauty of a landscape, or the intricate details of everyday life, my goal is to create photographs that resonate with you, that make you feel as if you are right there in the moment.
